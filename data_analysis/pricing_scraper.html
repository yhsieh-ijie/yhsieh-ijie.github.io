Wolt Pricing Scraper

The main objective of building a pricing scraper is to help the pricing team and our stakeholders to get real-time pricing data of our competitor in a more efficient and effective way. Previously, it was done by manually go to the website and check one by one the prices of each store in order to compare the prices. As it is a time-consuming process, our stakeholders requested our support to provide a solution that could be adopted and applied at scale. The data that should be provided include:

  • Delivery Fee of each store based on the input location
  • If the prices vary based on the distance from the user, what is the exact distance tiers and the corresponding Delivery Fee?
